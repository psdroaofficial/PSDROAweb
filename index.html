About PSDROA

The Peoples Socialist Democratic Republic of Amityans (PSDROA) is an independent student-led organization built by and for the students of Amity Private School, Sharjah.

It serves as a safe, collaborative space where Amityans can freely connect, discuss, and work together — without teacher involvement. PSDROA operates through Microsoft Teams as a self-governed republic of students, fostering open discussion, creativity, and unity.
